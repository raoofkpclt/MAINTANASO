MAINTANASO SEO FILES

Files:
- index.html: SEO-ready version of your uploaded HTML
- robots.txt: allows crawling and points to sitemap
- sitemap.xml: homepage sitemap

IMPORTANT:
This package currently uses:
https://www.maintanaso.com

If your real domain is different, replace every occurrence of:
https://www.maintanaso.com
with your actual HTTPS domain.

Also add:
- og-image.jpg (recommended 1200 x 630 px) to your public/root folder
- Google Search Console verification after deployment
- Google Business Profile for your real service area
- Exact city/service-area details in the page and structured data

SEO cannot guarantee #1 ranking. Rankings depend on competition, content,
local relevance, backlinks, reviews, site speed and Google indexing.
